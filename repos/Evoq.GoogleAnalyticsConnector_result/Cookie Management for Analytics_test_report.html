Key Findings

Critical Discovery: Legacy Analytics Cookies Disabled by Default

The legacy analytics cookies (Analytics_VisitorId, Analytics) are disabled by default in the current DNN configuration. This is controlled by the EnableLegacyAnalyticsCookies setting in web.config.

Impact: No visitor tracking cookies are being created for analytics purposes unless explicitly enabled.

To Enable: Add the following to web.config under <appSettings>:

<add key="EnableLegacyAnalyticsCookies" value="true" />
