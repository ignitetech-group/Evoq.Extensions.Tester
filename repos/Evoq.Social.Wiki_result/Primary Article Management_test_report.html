Observations

  • Primary Article Behavior: The primary article acts as the wiki module's landing page content. It is displayed prominently before the article list.
  • Title Field Suppression: When editing a primary article, the title field is intentionally hidden. The code uses the module title instead (ContentController.cs line 104).
  • Moderator Controls: The Delete button for primary articles is only shown to users with CanModerate permission (ContentEditor.ascx lines 117-126).
  • Change Primary Article: While the backend supports changing the primary article via SetPrimaryArticle(), direct UI access would typically be through module settings which was not easily accessible during testing.
  • Point System: Editing articles awards points to users (observed user points increasing from 114 to 120 during testing).
